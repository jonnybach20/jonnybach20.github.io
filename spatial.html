Spatial Data Analysis

Geographical Information Systems were a key component of Master's program. My modules taught me proficiency in QGIS, I learned how to digistise and manipulate spatial data, subjecting it to various types of data analysis. I receievd a distinction in this module without any prior expereince in GIS, demonstrating my ability to easily acquire new skills.

For my first assignment, I had to submit my solutions to solve various complex geospatial problems. The problems related the historic geospatial analysis made by John Snow in 1854 and solving them required the use of multiple techniques, such as, digitising, georeferencing, and the creation of raster layers, point vector layers, and polygon vector layers.

I was then tasked with creating a research poster as a way of scientifically communicating the effect climate change can have on the distribution of an invasive species, by using results from species distribution models.
