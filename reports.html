Report Writing

The aim of my Master's degree was to provide us with the training and skills to produce high-quality reports for a variety of purposes. My undergraduate background in Criminology with Psychology provided me with the foundation to utilise and improve upon my data collection and evidence gathering skills, to be successful in my Master's degree. My undergraduate degree is also, however, where I discovered my interest in ecology, environmental policy, and the decision-making that influences the planned environment. As seen within my undergrad report on environmental injustices, which was graded with a first.

My 'Climate Change, Science, and Impacts' module as part of my Master's degree aimed to provide us with an understanding of climate change science and data. For this module, I was tasked with manipulating climate scenario models and summarizing the data within an accesible and palatable report, which received a distinction grade.
