About me

I am a 23-year-old young professional who currently works as a Technical Support Officer within the Strategic Policy team for the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park Authority. I am from Pembrokeshire but I lived and studied in Bristol for four years.

I am an avid reader. I played guitar from a young age and I have a keen interest in music. I enjoy film and go to cinema whenever I can. I enjoy exercising regularly and weightlifting, my favourite sports are football and basketball.

I am a capable individual and a quick learner, in my current role I have quickly been improving my planning knowledge and my GIS skills. I enjoy learning new skills which I hope this admittedly amateur attempt at web design proves.

Navigating this webpage you will find links to some of my professional and academic experiences. Feel free to search for these from the home page!
